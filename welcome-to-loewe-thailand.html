How to say LOEWE

Founded in 1846 as a Madrid leather-making collective, LOEWE is now one of the world’s major luxury houses, known for artisanal craftsmanship, innovation, and unparalleled leather expertise.

Playing on our tricky to pronounce name, our latest film features Toey Jarinporn, Thanaerng Kanyawee, Jayler Krissanapoom, Peach Pachara, and rising stars Pat Chayanit and Phuwin Tangsakyuen.
